## üéØ **The Polytope Framework**

### **What is a Polytope?**
A pattern formed when 3+ red flags are detected across different risk dimensions.

### **Five Risk Dimensions**
1. **Volatility** - Daily price changes > 5%
2. **Market Cap** - Size below $500M threshold
3. **Valuation** - P/E or other ratio extremes
4. **Sector Risk** - High-risk industries
5. **Momentum** - Unusual price movements

### **Investment Signals**
- üü¢ **0 flags** = REVIEW - Proceed with analysis
- üü° **1-2 flags** = HOLD - Investigate further
- üî¥ **3+ flags** = DO NOT BUY - High risk pattern
